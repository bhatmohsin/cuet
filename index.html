About CUET

The Common University Entrance Test (CUET) is a standardized test for admission to undergraduate programs in central universities across India. It's crucial for students aiming for top colleges and universities.

Counselling Process

The CUET counselling process involves several steps:

  1. Registration
  2. Choice filling
  3. Seat allocation
  4. Document verification

Our expert guidance can help you navigate this process smoothly.
